Chicken Cobbler

Home

Description

This scrumptious chicken cobbler brings together the best of comfort food in one simple dish—a creamy shredded chicken base topped with golden-brown, buttery biscuit topping. After it became a viral sensation delighting TikTok audiences everywhere, we had to try the original recipe by @yourbarefootneighbor.

The one-pan ease of layering melted butter, shredded rotisserie chicken, frozen vegetables, creamy soup, milk, and boxed Red Lobster Cheddar Bay biscuit mix makes it a weeknight hero. Whether you’re after a quick-to-prep family dinner or a cozy potluck dish to feed a hungry crowd, this recipe ticks all the boxes!
